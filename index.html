ABOUT US

          In 2009, Sto. Niño Mactan Montessori School, Inc. was established by Mr. Charles U. Lim and Mrs. Sunshine B. Lim with the goal of delivering quality education to young learners. Initially offering pre-school education with 30 students and 4 teachers, the school quickly expanded, obtaining a government permit for Kindergarten in the following year, with 161 students and 8 teachers. Over the years, the school's success led to the acquisition of permits for elementary, junior high school, and senior high school levels by 2015. By 2018, the school had grown to accommodate over 380 students and 30 teachers, reflecting its commitment to continuous improvement and providing better education opportunities.

BIDLISIW

                    "Bidlisiw," a Cebuano-Visayan term for "ray of the sun," symbolizes a new beginning and fresh start, both literally and figuratively.
It marks the end of darkness and the start of a hopeful journey. As the official publication of SNMMSI, Bidlisiw offers solace and inspiration to students,
                           parents, teachers, and staff, reminding them of the good memories that make school life worthwhile despite challenges.




















MISSION


TO CHERISH

MISSION



TO HONE THE SKILLS

      The child in the areas of communication in oral and written. Mathematics, Science and the Performing Arts so that he/she will be confident in expressing his ideas and interaction with others

      Christian Virtues and Filipino values so that he/she may possess the basic understanding of what it means to be a family member, what it takes to sustain the Filipino values and what it behooves to attain and live Christian virtues and values

TO LEAD
